The On Shade Narrative

Founded on the principles of integrity and high performance, On Shade Transportation quickly established itself as the go-to partner for complex logistics in the rapidly developing UAE market. Our story is one of continuous investment in our people, technology, and fleet.

A Fleet Built for the Future (150+ Vehicles)

We maintain a state-of-the-art fleet of over 150 vehicles, ranging from light transport units to specialized multi-axle trailers and heavy-duty cranes. This modern arsenal ensures we have the capacity and versatility to meet any requirement, regardless of scale or urgency. All vehicles are tracked and maintained to the highest international standards, ensuring peak performance and minimizing road risk.

Unwavering Commitment to Safety

In the heavy logistics sector, safety is non-negotiable. Our operating procedures adhere strictly to local and international safety regulations. Every driver and heavy lift operator undergoes rigorous, ongoing certification. This stringent approach ensures every consignment—whether it's delicate cargo or critical infrastructure—is handled with the utmost care, protecting your assets and our team.
